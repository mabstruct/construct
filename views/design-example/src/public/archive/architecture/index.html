Design Principles

1
Map, not archive
Focus on connections and topology rather than document storage
2
Domain-agnostic infrastructure
Flexible architecture capable of tracking any research domain
3
Human-in-the-loop
Continuous feedback integration for system refinement
4
Transparent
Clear visibility into agent decisions and data sources
5
Evolving
Adaptive systems that improve through learning and reweighting
6
References over copies
Link to sources rather than replicate content
