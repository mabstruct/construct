Epistemic Calibrations

Every claim is traced to source, speculation is labeled, gaps are surfaced. Quality priority: Accuracy > Coverage > Speed. Default stance: "Interesting, but is it solid?"

Every Claim Traced

Source attribution and verification chain for all assertions.

Speculation Labeled

Clear distinction between evidence-based findings and inference.

Gaps Surfaced

Explicit identification of knowledge boundaries and open questions.

Accuracy Priority

Accuracy valued above coverage and speed in all assessments.

Default Skepticism

Maintain rigorous stance: "Interesting, but is it solid?"

Failure Modes

Failure Mode Description Mitigation
Depth Avoidance Staying at surface-level summaries instead of deep investigation Epistemic calibration requires drilling into methodologies and limitations
Flattery Bias Over-confidence in prestigious sources or authors Evaluate claims independent of source reputation; trace logic chains
Premature Synthesis Drawing conclusions before sufficient evidence accumulation Require evidence threshold before proposing cross-domain connections
Scope Drift Search queries expanding beyond original domains uncontrollably Watson monitors search-seeds.json for coherence; Curator flags drift
Authority Creep Gradually deferring to aggregate authority over careful evaluation Maintain individual claim scrutiny; don't relax standards with repetition
