Search Pattern Evolution

Eight major versions of search patterns, each triggered by specific observations and human direction.

v1: Initial Patterns

February 24, 2026
Triggers: System launch — 8 clusters, uniform weights, 3 terms per cluster
Foundational search seeds established across 8 ISW domains

v2: Intent-to-Action Focus

March 1, 2026
Triggers: First human feedback after 6 digest cycles — intent-to-action pipeline identified as key gap
Added intent-to-action cluster (weight 1.5). Boosted robot-learning, vision-language. De-emphasized perception-detection, foundations-training.

v3: Causal World Models

March 4, 2026
Triggers: Watson landscape analysis — world models everywhere but causal reasoning absent
Added causal-world-models cluster. Integrated interventionist prediction, counterfactual reasoning terms.

v4: Indoor World Model

March 7, 2026
Triggers: Embodied AI cluster too large (28 papers, 44%). Needed differentiation.
Split indoor-world-model into own cluster. Forced differentiation of digital twin and spatial representation work.

v5: Middleware + Spatial RAG

March 10, 2026
Triggers: Watson identified orchestration layer between planning and control as emerging field
Added agentic-robot-middleware and spatial-rag-robotics clusters. AgentRob, OpenClaw as anchors.

v6: Intent Decomposition

March 14, 2026
Triggers: 13 clusters, differentiated weights (0.5–2.0). Intent-to-action boosted to maximum.
Intent-to-action weight → 2.0 with 8 search terms. Perception and foundations de-emphasized to 0.5.

v7: Autonomous Simulation Loops

March 24, 2026
Triggers: Human direction — simulation as operational loop for learning, eval, regression, scenario generation
Added autonomous-sim-loops cluster (weight 1.3). Closed-loop simulation as instrument: scenario generation, regression testing, failure replay, synthetic data pipelines.

v8: Game Engine Sim Platforms

March 24, 2026
Triggers: Human direction — include game engine / simulation platforms in sim-loops search area
Expanded autonomous-sim-loops with platform-neutral sim terms: Unity, Isaac Sim, MuJoCo, Unreal, Habitat. 14 clusters total.
