Closed Gaps (since v1)

✓ Manipulation / Grasping

Partially addressed through Hierarchical VL Planning for humanoid manipulation and 3D-Grounded VL Framework for task planning.

Critical Open Gaps 🔴

● Intent-to-Action Pipeline

The new research focus. No dedicated papers yet. v2 pattern should address this. Status: Primary research target.

● Multi-Robot Coordination

No coverage of swarm/fleet/multi-robot systems. Critical gap in embodied AI ecosystem.

● Safety & Ethics of Embodied AI

Still absent from corpus. Essential for real-world deployment and research integrity.

● Outdoor Environments

Entirely indoor-focused. Need coverage of outdoor navigation, weather resistance, and environmental variability.

● Hardware / Sensors

No sensor-specific papers. Missing hardware constraints, sensor fusion, and device-specific optimizations.

Important Open Gaps 🟡

● Affordance Reasoning

How robots understand what actions are possible given physical state. Added as wildcard in v2.

● Sim-to-Real Transfer

Mentioned in several papers but no dedicated coverage of domain gap problem and transfer learning strategies.

● Human Intent Disambiguation

What happens when instruction is ambiguous? Intent clarification dialogue and multi-turn reasoning.

● Failure Recovery

What does the robot do when action fails? Replanning, error detection, and graceful degradation strategies.
