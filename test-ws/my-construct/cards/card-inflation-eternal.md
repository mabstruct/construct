## Summary
Eternal inflation is a consequence of many inflationary models where quantum fluctuations cause the inflaton field to climb back up its potential in some regions, leading to infinite expansion.

## Key Points
- Requires a region of the potential where the slow-roll parameter is small
- Produces a fractal-like multiverse structure
- Connects to string landscape through probability measure
- Predicts certain CMB signatures (non-Gaussianity, bubble collisions)
