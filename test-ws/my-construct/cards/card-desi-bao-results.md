## Summary
DESI Year-1 BAO measurements from 6 million galaxies show 2.5σ preference for evolving dark energy over ΛCDM.

## Key Findings
- Combined BAO + PantheonPlus + CMB: w₀ = −0.75 ± 0.13, wₐ = −0.83 ± 0.29
- 2.5σ tension with ΛCDM (w₀ = −1, wₐ = 0)
- First significant evidence for dynamical dark energy from a BAO survey
- Separate redshift bins show consistent deviations
