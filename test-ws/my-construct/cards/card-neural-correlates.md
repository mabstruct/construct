## Summary
Neural correlates of consciousness (NCC) are the minimal neural mechanisms jointly sufficient for any specific conscious experience.

## Key Findings
- Recurrent processing in visual cortex
- Global ignition in frontoparietal network
- Gamma-band synchronization
- Default mode network deactivation
- Distinction between content-specific and full NCC
