## Summary
Dark energy evolution is commonly parametrized by w(a) = w₀ + wₐ(1 − a), where w₀ is the present value and wₐ captures time variation.

## Key Points
- ΛCDM corresponds to w₀ = −1, wₐ = 0
- DESI Year-1 BAO shows 2.5σ preference for wₐ < 0
- Combined with Pantheon+ and CMB, the preference strengthens
- Systematic degeneracies between w₀ and wₐ require careful treatment
