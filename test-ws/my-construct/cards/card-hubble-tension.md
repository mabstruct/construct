## Summary
The Hubble tension is a 4–6σ discrepancy between local H₀ (~73 km/s/Mpc) and early-universe CMB inference (~67.4 km/s/Mpc).

## Evidence
SH0ES: H₀ = 73.04 ± 1.04. Planck: H₀ = 67.36 ± 0.54. JWST Cepheid observations confirm HST calibrations.

## Significance
The most pressing open problem in cosmology, pointing to possible new physics if not resolved by systematics.
