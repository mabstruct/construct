## Summary
Void lensing uses the gravitational lensing effect of cosmic voids to probe dark matter distribution and large-scale structure.

## Key Points
- Provides independent probe of structure growth
- Complements galaxy cluster lensing
- Sensitive to modified gravity signatures
- Enables CMB cold spot investigations
